This graphic uses data from Kaggle[24] that has Billionaires in the US in 2023 and the cities that they live in. We used length visual encoding in the form of a spike to show a clear indication to the reader which cities have the greatest number of billionaires. As you can see from looking at the graphic, major Tech hubs have a high concentration of billionaires such as New York City and San Francisco, California. It is also very clear that there is a lack of billionaires in parts of the United States such as the Midwest and southern western states.
